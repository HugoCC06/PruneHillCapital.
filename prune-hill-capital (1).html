Why we exist

Theory only gets
you so far.

Finance graduates understand frameworks. What they often can't do is build a portfolio, model a company, or manage risk under real conditions. Employers consistently flag this gap.

Prune Hill Capital exists to close it. No equivalent fund exists at Royal Holloway. We're building the track record and culture now — with a long-term goal of directing returns to RHUL student hardship support.

Who we are

A fund built by students, for students.

Prune Hill Capital was founded by Hugo Christie at RHUL's Business School to give students the practical investing experience that genuinely differentiates them — and to build something that outlasts any single cohort.

The fund operates under a formal Investment Mandate (v1.1), overseen by an Academic Adviser and Advisory Board. A succession policy governs leadership transitions, ensuring continuity regardless of graduations or departures.

As a non-profit, no student or staff member receives personal financial benefit. All gains remain within RHUL — with a defined pathway to student hardship support as the fund grows.

Timeline

Founded

Prune Hill Capital is established at RHUL. The investment mandate, governance structure, and succession policy are formalised from day one.

First Cohort

The founding leadership team is assembled. The first investment research cycle begins — from sector screening and memo writing to full Investment Committee pitches.

Growing

The fund expands its analyst base, strengthens RHUL Business School ties, and begins building partnerships with peer student funds at other UK universities.

Today

Building a track record and the institutional foundations needed to eventually manage real capital transparently — and direct returns to RHUL student hardship support.

What we invest in.
What we never will.

Approved by Academic Adviser · Royal Holloway, University of London

Permitted instruments
We invest in
  • Listed equities on regulated developed-market exchanges
  • ESG-screened ETFs and index funds
  • Cash and cash equivalents at the broker
Prohibited instruments & sectors
We never invest in
  • Derivatives, leverage, or margin — under any circumstances
  • Cryptoassets or unregulated digital tokens
  • Fossil fuels (exploration, production, refining, transport)
  • Armaments, tobacco, gambling, adult entertainment
  • Unlisted or private securities
